About Department

Department of Information Technology was started in 1998, offering B.Tech. (IT) programme under the affiliation of Madurai Kamaraj University. With excellent laboratory and research facilities, the programme was continuously affiliated by the Madurai Kamaraj University and later by Anna University. The Programme was accredited by NBA in the year 2004.

Information Technology deals with:

  • Design and Development of Computer Software and hardware.
  • Analysis Design and Maintaining Communication Systems.
  • Research and Development in emerging Computing Era.
  • Networking
